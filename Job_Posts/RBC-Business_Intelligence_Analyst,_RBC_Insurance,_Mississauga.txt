What is the job? 
Responsible for providing business intelligence/data analytics of insurance information through the creation and production of insurance reporting and analytics.  This is done by building/maintaining a MIS framework used to uncover trends/patterns through data mining and providing analytical support and client insight for all insurance divisions.
 
What will you do?
•             Contribute to the development and maintenance of data warehouse capabilities in SAS
•             Contribute as the centre of expertise in all manner of product-centric data mining activities including data analysis, aggregation and management
•             Participate in strategy meetings to gather and document business requirements, provide input and guidance based on analysis of results
•             Assist in conducting complex engineering of KPI’s and analytical studies to help drive effective portfolio management
•             Seek out new ways to engineer and manufacture best in class standard reports and dashboards to showcase insights and support tactics
•             Counsel the use and interpretation of custom built reports to highlight business intelligence
•             Manage day to day workflow and negotiate realistic timelines
•             Participate actively in self and peer development relating to financial products, operational processes, relationship management, and technology enhancement
•             Champion a positive, trusting, and satisfying work climate by living RBC values and culture
•             Ensure compliance and privacy guidelines are strictly adhered to
 
What do you need to succeed?
Required:
             Be effective and equally conversant in business and technical environments
             Solid working experience with very large relational databases and large volumes of data of various formats
             SAS and SQL programming demonstrating the use of efficient querying/datamining techniques
             Solid working experience with MS Office Professional Suite with emphasis on Visual Basic or Visual Basic for Applications, for customized application development and information presentation
             Knowledge of statistical procedures and applied data validation mechanisms
             Strong knowledge of Dashboard Reporting methodology
             Minimum undergraduate degree in Statistics/Mathematics, Finance, Computer Science, or a discipline that develops capability in data analytics and reporting
             Minimum 1 years experience working with financial products/services and corresponding data
             Strong analytical and problem solving skills
             Strong, innate, business and data acumen
 
Preferred:
•             Comfortable managing large quantities of data
•             Solid knowledge of unix/AIX v5, Terradata
